# Diogel Privacy Policy and Terms of Service

## Privacy Policy

Effective as 16 March 2026

1. Diogel is a Free Browser Extension for Chrome and Firefox, that does not collect or process any personal information
   or data from its users.

## Child safety standards

Diogel does not knowingly collect information from children. The extension has no age verification because it collects
no personal information from anyone. The application is 17+. We rely on Chrome Web store & Firefox Browser Addons age
verification to make sure the user downloading the app is an adult. Since we do not control which relays the user
connects to, there is no content moderation beyond the standard block post, block account, and report post and/or
account that will hide the content from the user.
